  1. Answer the following questions (and show your code): how many events were recorded in total? how many of those events are earthquakes? How many of the variables have the word error in their name? What is the average magnitude of the observed events?

There were 16,414 total events, of those, 16,282 were earthquakes. 3 of the variables have “error” in them (horizontal error, depth error and mag error). The average magnitude was 3.793.
